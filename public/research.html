Help & Instructions

How to use Keyword Research

  1. Enter a seed keyword and select your target country
  2. Use modifier chips to add "best", "how to", "buy" etc.
  3. Click Find Keywords to fetch Google Suggest results
  4. Select keywords you want or click Cluster All
  5. Export results as CSV or PDF

Country Filtering

Selecting a country biases Google results toward that region and filters out keywords mentioning other countries.

Intent Classification

  • Informational: how, what, why, guide, tutorial
  • Transactional: buy, price, order, cost
  • Commercial: best, review, compare, vs, top
  • Navigational: login, website, official, app
